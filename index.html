What is Hillsdale Hacks?

H-Hacks is a 24-hour hackathon, organized by Hillsdale High School's Girls Who Code Club, Hack Club, and Engineering Club.

How much does it cost?

H-Hacks is FREE for all attendees! We'll have food, wifi, workspace, swag, and prizes, so you can focus on building something awesome.

Do I need to know how to code?

No! During the hackathon, there will be workshops and mentors to help you get started with your first coding project.

FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an overnight event where students work in teams of four to make projects with code. At a hackathon, you'll meet fellow coders, interact with sponsors, and learn how to code projects that can change the world.

Who can attend?

All high school students are encouraged to attend Hillsdale Hacks! Whether you're a first time coder or seasoned hacker, H-Hacks welcomes you.

What should I bring?

Essentials:
*valid student ID
*laptop/computer
*chargers for all your devices
*reusable water bottle
Optional:
*sleeping bag

Is there a code of conduct?

Yes. All attendees are expected to follow the MLH Code of Conduct.
